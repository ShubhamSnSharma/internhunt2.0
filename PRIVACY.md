# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 25, 2024

## Introduction

InternHunt is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide
- **Resume Files**: PDF or DOCX files you upload for analysis
- **Job Preferences**: Location, role, and other search criteria
- **Chat Conversations**: Messages exchanged with our AI career assistant

### Automatically Collected Data
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the platform
- **Session Information**: Login times and activity logs (admin panel only)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:
- **Analyze Resumes**: Extract skills, experience, and qualifications using ML/NLP
- **Match Internships**: Provide personalized job recommendations
- **Recommend Courses**: Suggest relevant learning paths based on your profile
- **AI Assistance**: Power our Gemini-based career chatbot
- **Improve Platform**: Enhance our algorithms and user experience

## Data Storage & Security

- **Database**: Securely stored in Neon PostgreSQL (serverless, encrypted)
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- **Access Control**: Only accessible through secure admin authentication
- **Retention**: Resume data stored only while you use the platform

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- ❌ Sell your personal information
- ❌ Share data with third parties for marketing
- ❌ Use your data for purposes other than platform features

We **MAY** share data only:
- ✅ With your explicit consent
- ✅ When required by law
- ✅ To protect our rights and safety

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services:
- **Google Gemini API**: For AI chatbot functionality (subject to Google's privacy policy)
- **Neon**: For database hosting (subject to Neon's privacy policy)
- **Streamlit Cloud**: For application hosting (subject to Streamlit's privacy policy)
- **Vercel**: For landing page hosting (subject to Vercel's privacy policy)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your data
- **Correct**: Update inaccurate information
- **Export**: Download your data in a portable format

## Cookies & Tracking

- We use minimal cookies for session management
- No third-party tracking or advertising cookies
- Essential cookies only for platform functionality

## Children's Privacy

InternHunt is intended for users 16 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
